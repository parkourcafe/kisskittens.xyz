What the hour feels like

Permission to feel everything.

Behind the unmarked door: low amber light, the scent of neroli and cedar, a room kept exactly at body temperature. No screens, no clocks, no expectation but your own. Just warm oil, slow hands, and an hour where the only thing asked of you is to receive.

01

You set the edge

A quiet word before you begin maps exactly how far the hour goes. Yes is yes; everything else simply isn't.

02

Held, not hurried

Your therapist follows your breath, not a timer. The hour ends when your body says so.

03

Afterglow included

Tea, water, low light and time. You leave when you're ready — never a moment before.
