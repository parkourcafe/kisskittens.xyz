Our Founder
From one party to a movement

It started with a dare.

In 2005, a single masked party in London asked a simple question: what if women set every rule? What if desire could be celebrated without apology, and safety was never up for debate?

Word spread the way the best things do — quietly, then everywhere. One party became a calendar, a calendar became a community, and a community became a network spanning the globe.

Two decades on, we've thrown nights on four continents and built an app that carries the feeling anywhere. The rule hasn't changed: women come first.
