Our story

We built the room we wanted.

VELVET started as a question between friends: where do you go to meet someone real, when every app feels the same?

The idea

We believe connection deserves better than a feed. So we made a society where women are celebrated, men show up with intention, and every introduction is earned — never algorithm-ed.

— The founders of VELVET
Camille L. · Founder
From a dinner to a movement

It began with twelve chairs and a rule: be real.

In 2015, a private supper in London brought together a dozen people tired of small talk and swiping. One rule held the night together — leave the performance at the door.

Word spread quietly, the way the best things do. A dinner became a salon, a salon became a society, and the society became an app — so the feeling could travel without losing its discretion.

Today VELVET hosts nights across four continents, but the rule hasn't changed. Be real, be respectful, and let the evening surprise you.

What we stand for

Three things we never compromise

✦

Women, centred

The experience is designed around women's comfort, safety and agency. They set the pace; everyone else rises to it.

✦

Discretion, always

Verified identities, private profiles, no phones at the table. What happens inside the room belongs to the room.

✦

Intention over volume

We curate for genuine interest, not endless options. Fewer, better introductions — the kind worth your evening.
