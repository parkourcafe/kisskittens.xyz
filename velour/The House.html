How a visit unfolds

From the threshold to the afterglow — every visit follows the same gentle arc.

I

Arrive

A warm welcome, herbal tea, and a quiet conversation about what your body needs today.

II

Prepare

Your suite is warmed, the oil chosen, the light lowered. You undress to your comfort.

III

Surrender

Your therapist begins. From here, there is nothing to do but breathe and let go.

IV

Return

Tea, water, and time. You leave when you're ready — not a moment before.
